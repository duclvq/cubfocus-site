Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

AI Daily Friend is built with privacy as a core principle.

  • Your data never leaves your device. Your health metrics, location, calendar events, and journal notes stay on-device inside a LocalSnapshot that is never serialized to the network.
  • Only derived signals are sent to the cloud. To enrich your morning card, we send anonymized, derived signals only — such as sleep quality (poor/fair/good) and readiness level. Never raw numbers, never journal text, never exact location.
  • HealthKit. Sleep duration, heart rate, and HRV are read locally to calculate readiness. Nothing is stored outside HealthKit or shared externally.
  • Location. Used on-device only for geofence nudges (e.g. leaving home). Your coordinates are never sent to any server.
  • Calendar & Reminders. Read locally to build your daily schedule summary. Raw event data is never transmitted.
  • AI conversations. "Ask anything" sessions use the Claude API with a derived daily context payload — no personal identifiers, no raw data.
  • No advertising or analytics SDKs. AI Daily Friend contains no tracking frameworks of any kind.

For help or feedback, email leduchtth@gmail.com

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Cubfocus is designed with privacy as a core principle. Here is what you need to know:

  • No data collection. Cubfocus does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.
  • All data stays on your device. Your focus sessions, streaks, and stats are stored locally and never leave your device.
  • Camera usage. The app may request camera access to detect presence during focus sessions. No images or video are ever saved, uploaded, or processed off-device.
  • Health data. If you grant HealthKit access, data is read locally to personalize your experience. Nothing is stored or shared outside of HealthKit.
  • Notifications. Local notifications are sent on-device for streak reminders. No data is sent to external servers.
  • No third-party SDKs. Cubfocus contains no advertising, analytics, or tracking frameworks.

If you have any questions about privacy, contact us at leduchtth@gmail.com
