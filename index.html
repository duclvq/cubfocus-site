Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Cubfocus is designed with privacy as a core principle. Here is what you need to know:

  • No data collection. Cubfocus does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.
  • All data stays on your device. Your focus sessions, streaks, and stats are stored locally and never leave your device.
  • Camera usage. The app may request camera access to detect presence during focus sessions. No images or video are ever saved, uploaded, or processed off-device.
  • Health data. If you grant HealthKit access, data is read locally to personalize your morning briefing. Nothing is stored or shared outside of HealthKit.
  • Notifications. Local notifications are sent on-device for streak reminders and nudges. No data is sent to external servers.
  • No third-party SDKs. Cubfocus contains no advertising, analytics, or tracking frameworks.

If you have any questions about privacy, contact us at leduchtth@gmail.com
