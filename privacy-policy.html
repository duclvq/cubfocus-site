Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

scroll tracking ("the app") is built with your privacy as the top priority. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

The app does not collect any personal data. All information — including focus sessions, streaks, and settings — is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to any server.

2. Camera Usage

The app may request access to your device's camera during focus sessions to detect whether you are present. No images or video are ever captured, stored, or transmitted. Camera data is processed in real time on your device only and is discarded immediately.

3. Notifications

The app may send local push notifications (morning cards, reminders, nudges) based on your preferences. These notifications are generated entirely on your device. No notification content is sent to or processed by any external server.

4. Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any user data with third parties — because we do not collect any.

6. Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since no data is collected from any user, the app is safe for all ages.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the new version will be posted at this URL with an updated date.

8. Contact

Questions? Email us at: support@scrolltracking.app
